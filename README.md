## Project description

This project analyzes the AdventureWorks2022 database, which represents a global company engaged in the manufacture and sale of bicycles. The goal is to evaluate business performance in key areas such as sales, customers, products, and employee activity.


**Dataset:**

https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/sql/samples/adventureworks-install-configure?view=sql-server-ver17&tabs=ssms


I followed a simple workflow to explore and analyze the data:

**SQL:**


I downloaded the AdventureWorks database and restored it in SQL Server.
I started by joining multiple tables (sales, customers, products, employees).
I performed data aggregations to calculate key performance indicators, such as:

Sales trends by year and month,
Top customers by revenue and purchase frequency,
Best-selling and most profitable products,
Sales distribution by territory,
Employee sales performance.




**Power BI:**


Finally, I get the data from SQL Server into Power BI.
I used the results of the analysis in SQL to create interactive dashboards that allow for clear visualization of the information.
The visualizations include:

Sales trends over time,
Customer segmentation based on spending behavior,
Product performance in terms of revenue and profit margin,
Geographic distribution of sales,
Identification of top-performing employees.












**Personal Project Goals:**

The goal of this project was to apply SQL knowledge in a realistic business context, work with relational data models and complex queries and develop interactive dashboards to facilitate decision-making.
